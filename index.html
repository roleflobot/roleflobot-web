RoleFloBot™.
RoleFloBot™ is an innovative, AI-powered chatbot designed to enhance language learning through authentic, task-based roleplay. Developed from research published in the KOTESOL Journal, RoleFloBot™ integrates cutting-edge generative AI technology to deliver structured and immersive learning experiences.

Key features include dynamic scenario branching, real-time speech interactions, structured learning sequences, and immediate learner feedback.
